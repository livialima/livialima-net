My Personal Playlist: The Hits of October 2020

Listen on Spotify

Mariah Carey, also one of my favorite artists ever, decided to launch an album of rarities. That means a double-disc with awesome early-90's gems and an entire live show at the Tokyo Dome. She absolutely crushed it! That's it. That's the playlist. Some feel-good tunes and R&B smoothness. Enjoy!

  2. Trouble's Coming - Royal Blood
  3. Here We Go Around Again - Mariah Carey
  4. Show Them The Way - Steve Nicks
  5. A Ghost - Travis
  6. Slipping Away - Mariah Carey
  7. The Middle - Pomplamoose
  8. Concorde (Fab Remix) - Gregory Porter
  9. Fantasy (Live at the Tokyo Dome) - Mariah Carey
  10. Found Out - This Is The Kit
  11. Willow Tree (feat Jerome Farah) - Tash Sultana
  12. Vision of Love (Live at the Tokyo Dome) - Mariah Carey
  13. positions - Ariana Grande
  14. Crazy - Kelly Rowland
  15. Emotions (Live at the Tokyo Dome) - Mariah Carey
