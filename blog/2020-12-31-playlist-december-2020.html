My Personal Playlist: The (way too many) Top Hits of 2020

Listen on Spotify

You can say what you will about 2020, but no one have the courage to suggest it didn't have good music.

Come on, Taylor Swift released a surprise album. TWICE.

That little detail aside, the creatives answered the call of humanity and delivered insightful, emotional and honest works.

That's why I included entire albums in this playlist. It was not just a couple of singles, some artists were on top of their game and gave us masterpieces. Lots of pop drinking in the nostalgia pound with 70s and 80s influence, add some good Indie, a sprinkle of Rock, a couple of R&B gems and that's what 2020 sounded for me.

Enjoy and good 2021!
