Challenges

I do enjoy learning new things and sharing ideas. Therefore, I love a good challenge!

Challenges and puzzles are a way to exercise the mind and train the brain. These "mind exercises" allows people to stretch the limits of their mind and perception. It creates the perfect opportunity to think outside the box. It also encourages someone to gain knowledge about the subject at hand as well as the process they use to get there.

Here I document the challenges I go through and share my process with you. Enjoy!
