Useful Links, Bookmarks & Recomendations

Disclaimer: this list is a work in progress. I use most of the items here on a regular basis.

Apps

  • Headspace: mindfulness and guided meditation.
  • Wallet: finance tracker and budget planning.
  • GoWOD Mobility First: effective mobilization protocols, with demonstration videos, timer and mobility tests.
  • CrossFit btwb: official CrossFit workout tracker and the best I tried so far.
  • Lyrics Training: learn languages with music.
  • Forest: an interesting app to help you beat phone addiction and overcome distraction.
  • YourHour: phone addiction tracker and controller.
  • ScreenTime: another phone addiction tracker. I found this one a bit too intrusive for me but it can really help bad cases.

Books

  • Network Warrior by Gary Donahue is probably the single most important book in my career as a Network Analyst. It is not exam-centric like most of the popular books and it is not technology-centric either. It is WORK-centric, meaning it helps you do your job in the network support world. Straight to the point, Gary writes the way he talks and I get the feeling of mentorship whenever I read it.

Games

  • Elevate: collection of brain games for critical cognitive skills.
  • Lumosity: another collection of brain games, but it's stronger on the "game" side of it.
  • Flippy Bit And The Attack Of The Hexadecimals From Base 16: excellent game to practice your hexa conversion skills. Highly addictive.
  • Cisco Binary Game: first game I found to practice anything network related. It was a HUGE help in mastering subnetting.
  • Penjee Binary Game: adaptation of Cisco's Binary Game. It does not need any registering to play.
  • Penjee Binary Bonanza: another binary game.
  • Data Wing: I got obsessed with this game for a solid weekend, the time I took to finish it. More dedicated enthusiasts can do it faster, I'm sure. It's well designed and has a killer soundtrack.

Links

  • IP Address Guide: Free IP address tools allow you to ping an IP address, traceroute an IP address, convert IP address into decimal value or CIDR format, and so on for both IPv4 and IPv6 format.
  • Khan Academy: free, world-class education, specially in math.
  • Hemingway Editor: improve the readability of your articles.
